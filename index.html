Fintech Moldova Becomes a Partner of the Baltic Payments Forum to Connect Moldovan and Baltic Fintech Ecosystems

Introduction

Fintech Moldova is announcing its partnership with the Baltic Payment Forum, a major event focused on supporting the Ukrainian financial market and fostering international exchange of experience within the payment industry. The partnership will facilitate collaboration between the Baltic and Moldovan Fintech ecosystems, opening up the Moldovan finance sector to foreign service providers. The event will take place on April 25, 2023, at the Hilton Garden Inn City Center in Vilnius, Lithuania.

The Baltic Payment Forum aims to create a platform for financial leaders from European countries, Ukraine, and CIS countries to share best practices, learn about new trends and innovations in the payment industry, and find new partners to boost their businesses. The forum will address the challenges and opportunities emerging in the financial markets due to the increased volume of payments resulting from the displacement of Ukrainians abroad.

Fintech Moldova’s partnership with the Baltic Payment Forum is expected to drive growth in both the Baltic and Moldovan Fintech ecosystems. The forum will host a series of strategic sessions, panel discussions, and presentations that will cover topics such as European integration, cooperation in the payment sector, and the future of digital financial services. Invited guests include top experts and representatives from the European Banking Authority, National Bank of Ukraine, Bank of Lithuania, and Bank of Latvia.

Some of the key sessions include “Europe – Ukraine: Opportunities and Risks in New Economic Reality,” “Payments in a World Without Borders,” “Payment Experience,” and “Future in Finance: Digital Transformation of Banking.” Attendees will have the opportunity to engage in discussions, share their experiences, and explore potential partnerships with industry leaders from various countries.

Fintech Moldova’s involvement in the Baltic Payment Forum demonstrates its commitment to fostering innovation and collaboration within the finance sector. As a partner, Fintech Moldova will help shape the future of the industry and further integrate the Moldovan Fintech sector into the global market.

The Baltic Payment Forum promises to be a significant event for those involved in the finance and payment industries, providing valuable networking opportunities and access to the latest trends and innovations. For those interested in attending, you can register for the event on their website at https://payforum.tech/ . Be sure to use the code “Fintech MD” at checkout to receive a 10% discount on your registration.

Moldovan Startups Get a Chance to Shine at Seamless Middle East in Dubai

Powered by Fintech Moldova and the BRIDGE program, and in collaboration with Fintech Hub, up to 10 Moldovan startups will have the opportunity to showcase their innovative solutions at Seamless Middle East in Dubai this May. Seamless Middle East, a prestigious event now in its 23rd year, brings together the brightest minds and industry leaders in the fields of payments, fintech, banking, retail, e-commerce, digital marketing, home delivery, cards, and identity.

Moldovan startups that are younger than two years, have less than $1 million in revenue, and provide solutions in fintech, payments, identity, retail, e-commerce, home delivery, or digital marketing are encouraged to apply. This unique opportunity offers these startups a chance to introduce themselves to the vibrant Middle Eastern market and expand their reach.

The Bridge program will reimburse each selected startup for up to $1,500 in travel and accommodation expenses. The Future Technologies Activity, funded by USAID, Sweden, and UK Aid, and in partnership with the Invest Moldova Agency, launched the BRIDGE program to support local companies in accessing larger markets, acquiring know-how, establishing partnerships, and developing skills for integrating innovative technologies into their activities through participation in international events.

The deadline for applications is April 25th, so dont miss this chance to showcase your startup on a global stage and explore new opportunities in the dynamic Middle Eastern market. Apply now and seize the opportunity to take your business to new heights!

DTFI23 Conference: The Challenges and Opportunities of Digital Transformation for Financial Institutions.

Digital transformation has become increasingly important for financial institutions in order to stay competitive and relevant in todays fast-paced business environment. The Digital Transformation for Financial Institutions Conference (DTFI23 powered by Mastercard and maib), set to take place on March 24 in Chisinau, offers industry leaders and financial professionals an opportunity to learn about the latest technological advancements and strategies for digital transformation.

The conference will be a platform for attendees to network and share ideas about the digital landscape within the financial industry. They will hear from leading experts from Moldova, Romania, Georgia, Lithuania, and other countries who will be discussing topics such as the role of artificial intelligence in financial services, big data analytics in risk management, modernizing legacy systems, cloud computing, digital identity verification in financial transactions, open banking, digital-only banking, and cybersecurity best practices.

Irina Untila, Director of Mastercard office in Moldova, main partner of the conference, emphasizes the importance for financial institutions to embrace digital transformation:

“At Mastercard, we believe that digital transformation brings fundamental shift to the financial industry. And to succeed in this digital era, institutions should embrace innovative technologies and reimagine their customer experiences to create more seamless, secure and personalized solutions for their clients. Industry platforms, like DTFI23 Conference, provide an excellent opportunity for industry leaders to share best practices, and to explore new strategies to drive further growth, and we are proud to support this conversation for the second year in a row.”

Giorgi Shagidze, CEO of maib, main partner of the conference, highlights the importance for incumbent financial institutions to adopt innovation: “Innovation and digital transformation are key drivers in maib strategy. Digitalisation transforms our business activities and provides great opportunities for value creation. As the market rules are changing and the needs of our customers are evolving, bringing digital innovation and improvements in our business proposition has become a key focus in everything we do. We are happy to partner up with the conference, to share our experience and to learn from the best in this industry.”

Stefan Nistor, Founder and Coordinator for Fintech Moldova, organizer of DTFI23, points out the value of interacting with those who have successfully implemented innovative projects in Finance: “Digital transformation is not just about automating processes or delivering a better customer experience. Its about creating new products and services that were previously impossible. The possibilities are endless, and the conference is the perfect place to explore them.”

One of the most important themes to be discussed at the conference is the role of AI in digital transformation for financial institutions. AI has the potential to become a game-changer in the financial industry, allowing organizations to automate processes, improve accuracy, and deliver personalized customer experiences. AI can help financial institutions identify patterns in large datasets, provide more accurate risk assessments, and improve fraud detection.

Dont miss this chance to join the digital transformation movement in finance at DTFI23. Book a seat now at https://fintech.md/dtfi23/

It is composed of specialized software and algorithms that are used on computers and smartphones. Fintech, the word, is a shortened combination of “financial technology.”
